Macaroni Cheese

Description

Macaroni cheese brings together the joys of lovely italian pasta with a multitude of tasty cheeses.

Mac'n'cheese goes especially well with garlic bread on the side, or as a cheeky side of its own, next to ribs.

Ingredients

  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Butter
  • Milk
  • Flour
  • Macaroni
  • Cheddar
  • Parmesan
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Cayenne Pepper
  • Panko Breadcrumbs
  • Cherry Tomatoes

Method

  1. Firstly, finely chop the onion and garlic and sautee on a medium heat in the butter.
  2. Once softened, add a couple of tablespoons of flour, and cook for around a minute. This should create a paste-like roux.
  3. Add the milk to the pan, a little at a time, stirring constantly to prevent lumps forming. You should end up with a glossy, white sauce. Don't worry if it's not too thick yet, that comes when you add the cheese.
  4. While your beschemel is coming together, pop your pasta into a pan of salted, boiling water, and cook for around 10-11 minutes, until al dente.
  5. Add your cheese to the beschemel, on a low heat, until melted and combined.
  6. Season lightly with salt and plenty of pepper. You can add cayenne pepper to your liking.
  7. Drain the pasta and add into the sauce, mix until the pasta is fully coated.
  8. Pour into a baking dish, sprinkle over the panko breadcrumbs and add the cherries.
  9. Put the baking dish in the oven (180 degrees celsius) for approximately 15-20 minutes, until golden brown on top.
  10. Enjoy!
