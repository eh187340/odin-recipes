Egg Fried Rice

Description

Egg fried rice is an absolute staple, quick to make, relatively healthy, and very filling (and delicious).

It can be scaled up/down to your desires, and dependent on what you might have in the fridge!

Ingredients

  • Long grain rice
  • Spring onions
  • Garlic
  • Eggs
  • Sesame oil
  • Soy sauce

Method

  1. Firstly, get your rice on the boil. Use a large pan and always wash your rice first.
  2. Once the rice is cooked, drain and rinse with cold water.
  3. Finely slice your spring onions, and mince the garlic.
  4. Heat your wok until smoking. Add your sesame oil.
  5. Add the garlic and spring onions and stir fry until fragrant.
  6. Add the rice to the wok and stir constantly.
  7. Once the rice is warmed through, push to the side and crack the eggs in. Quickly mix the eggs to prevent them frying. Once set like a small omelette, you can chop them with your spoon and mix into the rice.
  8. Add a splash of soy sauce to your liking and mix well.
  9. Voila!
