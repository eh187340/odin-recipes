Beans on Toast

Description

Beans on toast doesn't have to be boring, and frankly, you can add just about anything.

This recipe will elevate the humble bean, and add some spice to your life.

Ingredients

  • Beans
  • Bread
  • Tomatoes
  • Fresh chillies
  • Smoked Paprika
  • Worcestershire sauce
  • Cider Vinegar
  • Sriracha
  • Pepper
  • Mustard(optional)

Method

  1. First, slap your beans on to warm in a small pan, on a medium-low heat.
  2. Chop up the fresh chillies and pop them in with the beans.
  3. Finely dice the tomatoes, pop them in too.
  4. Now comes the fun bit. Add all your spicy ingredients (everything not already in the pan). And mix well.
  5. Once they're bubbling, pop your toast on - no need for butter.
  6. Put your toast on a plate, and slather with a hefty pile of your beany concoction.
  7. Sleep on your own tonight, nobody else deserves the aftermath.
